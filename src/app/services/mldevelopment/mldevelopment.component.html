How does JobRocket benefit job seekers?

JobRocket helps to speed-up the job search process. After uploading their CV, seekers can search for jobs in the JobRocket database (which includes jobs posted directly to the JobRocket website and major UK job searching sites).

The unique JobRocket machine learning algorithm then instantly evaluates key elements in the CV against each job position and generates constructive feedback to help tailor the CV to the job being applied for, helping to bypass Applicant Tracking Systems used by employers when screening applications.
